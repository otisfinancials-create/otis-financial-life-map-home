### 5. CC BILLING CYCLE — Forecast Module
This is the most complex item. Implement the credit card billing cycle grouping in the forecast ledger:
Logic:

Any bill where Payment Method = "Credit Card" should NOT appear in the forecast on its individual due date
Instead, find which credit card it is paid by (matched to a Connected Account of type Credit Card)
Group all bills paid by that credit card that fall within the CC billing cycle (between cc_cycle_start_date and cc_cycle_end_date) under a single forecast group
The group appears in the forecast on the cc_payment_due_date of that credit card

Forecast display format for CC groups:
[Date] — Credit Card Due — [Card Name]
  Credit Card Payment         $0.00          [running balance]
    ↳ Netflix                 $15.99
    ↳ Spotify                 $9.99
    ↳ Electric Bill           $187.50
    ↳ Cell Phone              $291.00
Behavior:

The "Credit Card Payment" row starts at $0.00
When a user marks any individual line item below it as "Paid", the Credit Card Payment row increments by that amount
Example: user marks Netflix as paid → Credit Card Payment becomes $15.99
The running balance in the forecast only decreases when the Credit Card Payment row itself is updated — not when individual items are marked paid
Individual items can be expanded/collapsed under the CC Payment row
Note in a comment: "Plaid will automate this in a future phase — this is the manual version"
