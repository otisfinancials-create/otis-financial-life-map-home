Please build the Otis section at /otis. This is the heart of the app — a combined AI financial advisor and what-if simulator that lets users explore their financial future through conversation or pre-built scenario cards. This feature is Otis's biggest differentiator from every competitor.
### The Experience
When a user lands on /otis they see:

A warm greeting at the top: "Hi [name], what's on your financial mind today?"
A text input for Ask Otis (conversational mode)
A 4x3 grid of scenario cards below with the heading "Or explore a scenario:"

The page feels like a financial advisor's office — warm, smart, and personal. Not like a chatbot. Not like a calculator.
---
### Part A: Ask Otis (Conversational AI Mode)
A chat interface at the top of the page where users type questions in plain English:
Examples of what Otis can answer:

"What happens to my retirement if I take a $15K pay cut?"
"Can I afford a kitchen remodel next year?"
"What if I paid an extra $300/month on my mortgage?"
"How long until I pay off all my debt?"
"Am I on track to retire at 62?"
"What would happen if I lost my job for 3 months?"

Implementation:

Use the Anthropic Claude API (claude-sonnet-4-6) for all AI responses
Before sending to Claude, build a context object containing the user's real financial data:

Current account balances
Monthly bills and total monthly expenses
Pay schedules and monthly income
Loan balances, interest rates, and monthly payments
Retirement savings and monthly contributions
Net worth (assets minus liabilities)
Life events and their costs
Forecasted cash flow for next 12 months


Send this context to Claude with a system prompt that says: "You are Otis, a warm, smart, and deeply personal financial advisor. You have access to the user's complete financial picture. You give clear, specific, actionable answers based on their real numbers — not generic advice. You are encouraging but honest. You explain things simply without jargon. When running what-if scenarios, always show the specific dollar impact on their monthly cash flow, net worth, and retirement projection. You have the personality of a loyal, intelligent dog who always has their owner's best interests at heart — friendly, attentive, and always making eye contact. Your answers are concise but complete. Never recommend specific investments or give regulated financial advice."
Display Otis's responses in a clean chat bubble format
Show a subtle animated paw print 🐾 while Otis is thinking
Keep the last 10 messages in conversation history so Otis remembers context within the session
Add a "Start New Conversation" button to clear the chat

### Part D: Scenario Results Panel
After the user fills in a scenario form and clicks "Run Scenario", show a results panel with:
1. Impact Summary Cards (3 cards side by side):

*Monthly Cash Flow Impact: "+X"or"−X" or "-
X"or"−X per month"*
*Net Worth Impact at 1 Year: "+X"or"−X" or "-
X"or"−X"*
Retirement Impact: "X months earlier/later" or "$X more/less at retirement"

2. Before vs After Chart: A simple side-by-side or overlaid line chart showing:

Current trajectory (gray line)
With this scenario (colored line — green if positive, orange if negative)
Time range: next 5 years

3. Otis Commentary: A 2-3 sentence plain English summary from Otis explaining the impact. Use the Claude API to generate this dynamically based on the actual numbers. Example: "Taking a $15K pay cut would reduce your monthly cash flow by $1,250. At your current spending level, you'd start drawing down savings after 4 months. The good news — your retirement timeline only shifts by about 8 months if you keep your current contribution rate."
4. Save Scenario button: Saves the scenario with a user-defined name to a scenarios table
5. "Ask Otis about this" button: Pre-fills the chat with the scenario details so the user can have a deeper conversation

### Part G: API Routes

POST /api/otis/chat — send message to Claude API with user financial context, return response
POST /api/otis/scenario — run a specific scenario calculation, return impact numbers
GET /api/scenarios — list saved scenarios
POST /api/scenarios — save a scenario
DELETE /api/scenarios/[id] — delete a scenario

### Design Notes
This page should feel different from every other page in the app — warmer, more conversational, more alive:

The chat area should have a subtle different background — maybe a very light warm gray vs the white of the rest of the app
Otis's chat bubbles should have a distinct color — the teal accent
The paw print 🐾 appears subtly throughout — in the thinking animation, in the empty state, in the Otis commentary cards
Scenario cards should have a gentle hover animation — slight lift and shadow
The results panel should animate in smoothly when a scenario is run
Empty state message when no conversation yet: "Ask me anything about your financial future. I know your numbers and I'm here to help. 🐾"

Use the Anthropic Claude API (claude-sonnet-4-6) for all AI responses. The ANTHROPIC_API_KEY environment variable is already saved in Secrets. Use recharts for all charts. Use Tailwind for all styling consistent with the rest of the app
