Please build the Retirement section at /retirement. This section gives users a clear picture of where they stand today against their retirement goals, and projects whether they're on track based on current savings, contributions, and expected growth.
### Part A: Retirement Overview Page at /retirement
Show a summary panel at the top with 4 cards:

Current Retirement Savings (total across all retirement accounts)
Projected Value at Retirement (based on current contributions and growth rate)
Monthly Contribution (total across all accounts)
Retirement Readiness Score (simple percentage — projected value ÷ retirement goal × 100, shown as a colored progress bar: red <50%, orange 50-80%, green 80%+)

### Part B: Retirement Settings Form
A clean settings panel where the user enters their retirement assumptions:

Current age (number)
Planned retirement age (number — default 65)
Retirement goal amount (number — how much they want saved by retirement)
Expected annual return rate (% — default 7%)
Expected inflation rate (% — default 3%)
Monthly retirement spending goal (number — what they want to spend per month in retirement)
Social Security estimated monthly benefit (optional number — from SSA.gov)
Expected retirement duration (number of years — default 25)

Store these in the user_settings table (add columns if needed).
### Part C: Retirement Accounts List
Show all accounts tagged as "Retirement" type from Connected Accounts with:

Account name and institution
Current balance
Monthly contribution amount (add this field to the account if not already there)
Account type badge (401k, IRA, Roth IRA, Pension, Other)
A simple "Edit Contribution" button to update monthly contribution amount

If no retirement accounts exist, show a friendly empty state: "Add your retirement accounts in Connected Accounts to get started."
### Part D: Retirement Projection Chart
A line chart showing projected retirement savings growth from today until retirement age:

X axis: years (current age to retirement age)
Y axis: dollar amount
Two lines:

"On Track" line — projected value if contributions and growth rate continue
"Goal" line — flat horizontal line showing retirement goal amount


If the projected line crosses above the goal line — show a green "On Track" badge
If projected never reaches goal — show a red "Gap" badge and the dollar amount of the shortfall
Add a tooltip on hover showing age, year, and projected balance at that point

### Part E: What-If Retirement Scenarios (inline)
A simple panel below the chart with three sliders:

"What if I increase my monthly contribution by $X?"
"What if I retire at age X instead?"
"What if my annual return is X% instead?"

When any slider moves, instantly recalculate and update the projection chart in real time showing the new projected line alongside the original. Show the impact summary: "Retiring at 67 instead of 65 adds $X to your projected savings."
### Part F: Retirement Income Summary
A clean breakdown showing expected monthly income in retirement:

From retirement savings (projected monthly withdrawal using the 4% rule)
From Social Security (if entered)
Total monthly retirement income
vs. Monthly spending goal
Show surplus or shortfall clearly: "Your projected retirement income covers X% of your monthly goal"

### Part G: API Routes

GET /api/retirement/summary — return current savings, projected value, readiness score
GET /api/retirement/projection — return year-by-year projection data for chart
POST /api/retirement/settings — save retirement assumptions
GET /api/retirement/settings — retrieve retirement assumptions
